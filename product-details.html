Details

Ideal for studio, film, TV, and especially location shooting, the 3.57 x 2.6 x 2.6" MOLUS G60 Pocket COB Monolight from Zhiyun has COB LEDs and boasts a host of professional features in a very small package. The G60 has a wide variable color range of 2700 to 6500K with high CRI/TLCI 96/97 ratings and dims from 0 to 100%. Color and brightness can be adjusted on the fixture, but you can also make changes wirelessly using Zhiyun's app.

The G60 includes a multi-voltage AC adapter, power adapter organizer bag, and reflector. With the purchase of an optional USB-C charging cable, you can use PD technology to quickly power an optional attached battery for wireless use.
